Empowering Sustainable Progress

Since 2016, Parumaka (Pvt) Ltd has been at the forefront of innovation in Agriculture, Renewable Energy, and General Trading. Headquartered in Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka, we are driven by a mission to revolutionize industries through sustainable practices and empower communities nationwide.

  • Sustainable seed potato cultivation & grow bag farming systems
  • Clean energy solutions to reduce environmental impact
  • Quality-driven general trading for local and international markets
